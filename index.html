In “Naruto”, the viewers are introduced to the protagonist who is named Naruto Uzumaki. In the beginning, Naruto Uzumaki was a kid who was reckless and caused trouble to the grown-ups not caring about the consequences. He is proven troublesome toward the grownups while he lived in the village. In the anime, Naruto has blonde hair that is spiky and blue eyes. He also has some form of whiskers on the sides of his face. He wore an orange jumpsuit and wore a pair of goggles on his head to substitute as a head protector that is given to shinobis when they pass a test in school.
